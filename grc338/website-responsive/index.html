Welcome to Low Fidelitea.

Low fidelity, High quality.

Low fidelity or 'lo-fi' is "music that is recorded with intentional imperfections." This could be misplayed notes, environmental noise, low hum, or phonographic audio imperfections. Lo-fi music has become notorious for helping students all over the globe with studying. Its relaxing effects, lack of lyrics, and predictable tempo make it enjoyable without being a distraction during those hard grind times.

Low Fidelitea's main mission is to hone in on the benefits of lo-fi and provide a relaxing environment for ours visitors to vibe in. Come join us and enjoy some of the finest lo-fi beats and bubble tea in town.
